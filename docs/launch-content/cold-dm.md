# 💬 Cold DM Templates

**Framework**: Curiosity Gap + Genuine Curiosity Approach
**Emotional Trigger**: Vanity (they'll feel seen)
**Best time to send**: Weekdays 10 AM – 1 PM in recipient's timezone
**Engagement tips**: Only DM people you've already interacted with (liked/replied to their content). Never send the same template to more than 10 people in a day — Twitter will flag you. Follow up once after 3 days, then stop. Personalize the [OPENER] — reference something specific about their product or recent tweet.

---

## Template 1 — For Indie Hackers on Twitter

```
[Hey [NAME], loved your post about [SPECIFIC THING THEY POSTED]. ]
Quick question — I built a tool that runs 5 AI agents against any website 
and spits out a growth strategy in 60 seconds. Would you be down to try it 
on [THEIR PRODUCT] and tell me if the output is actually useful? 
Totally free — cmo-five.vercel.app
```

## Anti-Pattern Reminders

❌ Don't: "Hi! I built an amazing AI tool that's going to change marketing forever!"
❌ Don't: "Would love to get your feedback" without any personalization
❌ Don't: Send the DM cold — always warm up by engaging with their content first
❌ Don't: Follow up more than once
✅ Do: Ask a genuine question they'd want to answer even without your product
✅ Do: Make the ask about THEM (try it on YOUR product, give ME YOUR honest take)
✅ Do: Keep it under 3 sentences of core ask
