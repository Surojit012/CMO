Hey everyone 👋

I'm [YOUR NAME], and I built CMO because I was drowning in my own marketing homework.

**The problem**: I'm a solo builder. I can ship product fast — but every day I'd spend 3-4 hours doing the marketing work I knew I *should* be doing. Reading SEO guides. Rewriting CTAs. Lurking in Reddit threads looking for places to mention my product. Writing tweet threads that got 12 impressions.

I wasn't bad at marketing. I was just *slow* at it. And every hour spent on growth was an hour not spent on building.

**The "aha" moment**: I realized I kept doing the same 5 types of analysis every time — SEO keyword research, copy audit, conversion review, distribution mapping, and strategic positioning. These are distinct disciplines. A single AI chatbot trying to do all five produces generic mush.

So I built 5 separate AI agents, each one specialized, each one loaded with real marketing frameworks (LIFT Model, PAS, AIDA, 4 U's). They run in parallel and finish in about 60 seconds.

**What makes CMO different from tools like Okara.ai**:
- It's not monitoring — it's analysis + execution
- One-click content generation (tweet threads, blog posts, ad copy)
- Reddit monitoring that finds real conversations about your product category
- Autonomous mode that runs daily audits automatically
- Pay-per-use ($5 USDC) instead of monthly subscriptions

3 free analyses with full feature access. No credit card. No signup wall.
