# Copywriting Skill

## Frameworks

### 1. PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution)
The most reliable direct-response formula:
- **Problem**: Name the specific pain the reader is experiencing
- **Agitate**: Twist the knife — what happens if they DON'T fix it?
- **Solution**: Present the product as the obvious fix

Example structure:
> "Tired of [PROBLEM]? Every day you wait, [AGITATION]. [PRODUCT] fixes this by [SOLUTION]."

### 2. AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
Classic framework for ads and landing page copy:
- **Attention**: Pattern interrupt — bold claim, surprising stat, or provocative question
- **Interest**: "Here's why this matters to you..."
- **Desire**: Paint the outcome — what life looks like AFTER
- **Action**: Clear, urgent, low-friction CTA

### 3. Before-After-Bridge (BAB)
- **Before**: The world as it is now (painful)
- **After**: The world as it could be (desirable)
- **Bridge**: How to get there (your product)

### 4. The 4 U's for Headlines
Every headline must score high on:
- **Useful**: Does it promise a benefit?
- **Urgent**: Is there a reason to read NOW?
- **Ultra-specific**: Does it use concrete numbers/details?
- **Unique**: Is this a fresh angle?

### 5. Star-Story-Solution
For social and brand storytelling:
- **Star**: Introduce a relatable character (the customer)
- **Story**: Their struggle with the problem
- **Solution**: How they discovered and benefited from your product

### 6. Hook Categories (for social media virality)
- **Contrarian**: "Stop doing X. Here's why."
- **Curiosity Gap**: "This one thing changed how I..."
- **Social Proof**: "100K+ founders use this to..."
- **Fear of Missing Out**: "Your competitors already know this..."
- **Identity**: "If you're a [ICP], you need to know..."
- **Numberd**: "3 reasons your landing page isn't converting"
- **Hot Take**: "[Popular opinion] is dead. Here's what's next."

### 7. Emotional Trigger Map
Match copy angle to the dominant emotion:
| Emotion | Trigger Words | Best For |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Fear | "Don't miss", "Before it's too late", "Avoid" | Urgency campaigns |
| Greed | "Free", "Save", "Exclusive", "Secret" | Offers/deals |
| Vanity | "Stand out", "Be the first", "Ahead of" | Premium products |
| Authority | "Proven", "Research shows", "Trusted by" | B2B/SaaS |
| Belonging | "Join", "Community", "Together" | Community products |

### 8. Power Word Banks
- **Action**: Launch, ignite, crush, dominate, transform
- **Emotion**: Effortless, devastating, jaw-dropping, game-changing
- **Specificity**: Step-by-step, proven, exact, 7-minute, $0-cost

## Copy Rules
1. Lead with benefits, not features
2. Use "you" more than "we"
3. One idea per sentence
4. Short paragraphs (1-3 lines)
5. Concrete > Abstract ("saves 4 hours/week" > "saves time")
6. Kill adverbs — use stronger verbs instead
7. Read it aloud — if you stumble, rewrite

## Output Format
When generating copy, provide:
- The framework used (e.g., "PAS")
- The emotional trigger targeted
- 2-3 variations ranked by aggressiveness
- A "safe" version and a "bold" version
