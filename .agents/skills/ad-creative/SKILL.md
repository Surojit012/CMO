# Ad Creative Skill

## Frameworks

### 1. The Ad Creative Matrix
For each ad, define:
| Element | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Hook Type** | Question / Bold Claim / Social Proof / Pain Point / Curiosity Gap |
| **Visual Style** | UGC / Product Shot / Before-After / Screenshot / Meme |
| **Offer** | Free Trial / Discount / Lead Magnet / Demo / Waitlist |
| **Urgency** | Time-limited / Scarcity / Social proof ("X people signed up today") |
| **CTA** | Direct ("Try Free") / Benefit ("Get Results") / Curiosity ("See How") |

### 2. Platform-Specific Ad Formats

#### Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads
- **Primary Text**: 125 chars visible, 3 lines before "See More"
- **Headline**: 40 chars max
- **Description**: 30 chars max
- **Best Formats**: Video (15-30s), carousel, single image
- **Rule**: Lead with the hook in first 3 seconds of video / first line of text

#### Google Ads
- **Headline 1**: 30 chars — include primary keyword
- **Headline 2**: 30 chars — unique value prop
- **Headline 3**: 30 chars — CTA or social proof
- **Description 1**: 90 chars — expand on value, include keyword
- **Description 2**: 90 chars — features, trust signals
- **Rule**: Each headline should work standalone AND together

#### Twitter/X Ads
- **Copy Length**: 280 chars max, best under 100
- **Best Formats**: Conversational, hot take, thread-style
- **Rule**: Sound like a person, not a brand

#### LinkedIn Ads
- **Copy Length**: 150 chars for sponsored content intro
- **Best Formats**: Document (carousel PDF), single image with stats
- **Rule**: Lead with business outcome or data point

### 3. The 3-3-3 Ad Testing Framework
For every campaign, create:
- **3 Hooks**: Different opening angles (pain, gain, curiosity)
- **3 Offers**: Different value propositions or incentives
- **3 Audiences**: Different targeting segments

Test all 27 combinations (3×3×3). Kill losers after 48 hours. Scale winners.

### 4. Ad Copy Formulas

#### Formula 1: Pain → Empathy → Solution
> "Still [painful situation]? We get it — [empathy statement]. That's why [product] [solution]."

#### Formula 2: Social Proof → Benefit → CTA
> "[Number] [audience] already use [product] to [benefit]. [CTA]."

#### Formula 3: Before/After
> "Before [product]: [painful state]. After [product]: [desired state]. Try it free."

#### Formula 4: Objection Buster
> "Think [product] is [common objection]? [Counter with proof]. [CTA]."

#### Formula 5: The Hot Take
> "[Controversial opinion about industry]. Here's what smart [audience] do instead: [product angle]."

### 5. Creative Performance Metrics
Track for every ad:
- **CTR**: Click-through rate (benchmark: >1% Meta, >3% Google)
- **CPC**: Cost per click (industry-dependent)
- **Hook Rate**: % who watch past 3 seconds (video ads)
- **Thumb-Stop Rate**: % who stop scrolling (Meta)
- **Conversion Rate**: Clicks → desired action

### 6. Ad Fatigue Detection
Replace creatives when:
- CTR drops >20% from peak
- Frequency > 3x per user per week
- CPA increases >30% WoW
- Same creative running >14 days

## Output Format
When generating ad creatives, provide:
1. **Platform**: Where this ad should run
2. **Hook**: The attention-grabbing opener
3. **Body Copy**: Full ad text
4. **CTA**: The call to action
5. **Visual Direction**: What the image/video should show
6. **Targeting Suggestion**: Who should see this ad
