# AI-SEO Skill

## Frameworks

### 1. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
Optimize for AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE):
- **Structured Data**: Use bullet points, numbered lists, tables — AI models extract these easier
- **Direct Answers**: Start sections with clear, concise answers to the question
- **Entity Relationships**: Mention related entities, products, and concepts to build topical authority
- **Citations**: Include data points, statistics, and attributable quotes
- **Freshness Signals**: Update content regularly with dates and timestamps
- **FAQ Blocks**: Use structured FAQ schema that AI models can surface

### 2. Programmatic SEO Strategy
Scale content with templated pages:
- **Pattern**: [Location/Product/Use-case] + [Template]
- Example templates:
  - "Best [product category] for [audience/use-case]"
  - "[Product] for [industry]"
  - "[Tool A] vs [Tool B] — Which is better for [use case]?"
- **Data Source**: Use APIs, databases, or scraped data to populate unique variables
- **Quality Gate**: Each page must have >200 words of unique content beyond the template

### 3. Topical Authority Map
Build content clusters:
1. **Pillar Page**: Comprehensive guide (2,000-5,000 words)
2. **Cluster Posts**: 5-15 supporting articles targeting long-tail keywords
3. **Internal Link Web**: Every cluster post links to the pillar and 2+ siblings
4. **Hierarchy**: Pillar → Sub-topic → FAQ/comparison → Tutorial

### 4. AI-Assisted Content Workflow
1. Use AI to generate keyword clusters from a seed topic
2. Validate with search volume data
3. Generate content outlines (not full drafts)
4. Human-edit for brand voice, accuracy, and unique insights
5. Add original data, screenshots, or expert quotes AI can't generate
6. Optimize for featured snippets with structured formatting

### 5. E-E-A-T Optimization
Google's quality signals:
- **Experience**: Show first-hand experience with the product/topic
- **Expertise**: Include author credentials, bios, and expert quotes
- **Authoritativeness**: Get cited by other authoritative sources
- **Trustworthiness**: Accurate info, HTTPS, privacy policy, contact page

### 6. Search Intent Mapping
For every keyword, classify by intent:
| Intent | Signal | Content Type |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Informational | "how to", "what is", "guide" | Blog, tutorial, video |
| Navigational | brand name, specific product | Homepage, product page |
| Commercial | "best", "vs", "review", "top" | Comparison, review, list |
| Transactional | "buy", "pricing", "free trial" | Landing page, pricing page |

## Output Format
When providing AI-SEO recommendations, include:
1. **GEO Quick Wins** (optimize for AI answer engines today)
2. **Programmatic SEO Opportunities** (scalable content templates)
3. **Topical Authority Gaps** (content clusters to build)
4. **E-E-A-T Improvements** (credibility signals to add)
